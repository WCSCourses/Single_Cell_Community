Single-Cell (SinCell) LATAM Community

A non-commercial network of researchers and scientists committed to advancing single-cell biology in Latin America—through connections, training, shared resources, and collaborations.

Membership

Eligibility

  • Researchers and scientists based in Latin America. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Researchers and scientists outside LATAM with active collaborations/initiatives in the region (including diaspora). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
  • A genuine research interest in single-cell biology. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

How membership works

  • Automatic inclusion: past participants of the Rio, Colombia, and Costa Rica single-cell courses. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
  • Extended inclusion: participants of other LATAM courses (e.g., cancer genomics, organoids) who expressed interest in single-cell. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
  • Referral: each member may nominate one new person per year. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
  • Direct application: researchers from groups without prior Wellcome Connecting Science single-cell course participants may apply; reviewed case-by-case. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

Participation expectations

Members are encouraged to engage actively—for example by introducing themselves on the community platform, and optionally giving a short (3-minute) presentation in a webinar or special session. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}
