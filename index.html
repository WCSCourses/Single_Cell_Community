About

SinCell LATAM strengthens single-cell research capacity in Latin America by connecting researchers, sharing opportunities, and enabling collaboration in a trusted and inclusive space.

Connect the region

Build relationships across labs and countries.

Share training & resources

Promote learning and practical support.

Enable collaboration

Support initiatives across the region.

Membership

Eligibility

  • Researchers based in Latin America
  • Researchers outside LATAM with active collaborations in the region
  • A genuine research interest in single-cell biology

Pathways

  • Automatic inclusion via selected courses
  • Extended inclusion via related LATAM courses
  • Referral by existing members
  • Direct application (case-by-case)

Participation & conduct

Members are encouraged to participate actively and help maintain a respectful, inclusive environment.
