About

SinCell LATAM exists to strengthen single-cell research capacity in Latin America by connecting researchers, sharing opportunities, and enabling collaborations in a trusted, inclusive space.

Connect the region

Build relationships across labs, countries, and disciplines in LATAM.

Share training & resources

Promote learning, methods, and practical support for single-cell work.

Enable collaborations

Support initiatives that expand research capacity across the region.

Membership

We aim to keep the community open and welcoming while maintaining a safe, high-trust space.

Apply via form ↗

Eligibility

  • Researchers and scientists based in Latin America.
  • Researchers and scientists outside LATAM with active collaborations/initiatives in the region (including diaspora).
  • A genuine research interest in single-cell biology.

Pathways to join

  • Automatic inclusion: past participants of selected single-cell courses.
  • Extended inclusion: participants of other relevant LATAM courses who expressed interest in single-cell.
  • Referral: each member may nominate one new person per year.
  • Direct application: researchers may apply; reviewed case-by-case.

Participation

Members are encouraged to engage actively—for example by introducing themselves on the community platform, and optionally giving a short presentation in a webinar or special session.

Code of conduct

SinCell LATAM is a non-commercial, inclusive, and respectful space for scientific exchange. Breaches of conduct may result in suspension or removal.

Professional behaviour

Be respectful, constructive, and collegial in all community spaces.

Non-commercial

No sales, promotions, or commercial solicitation.

Stewardship

A steering group may approve, deny, or remove memberships to preserve integrity and manageability.
